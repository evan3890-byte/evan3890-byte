### Hi, I'm Mingtao He (何明涛)

PhD student at **Peking University** — Hittitology & Ancient Near Eastern Studies.

I study institutional texts from the Hittite Empire (~1650–1180 BCE) through the lens of legal hermeneutics, focusing on authorization chains, institutional silence, performativity, and interpretive competition.

Before academia, I taught AP English for years. The shift from classroom to cuneiform was less of a leap than you'd think — both are about decoding structured meaning from ambiguous signals.
